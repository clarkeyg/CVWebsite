FAQ

Questions, answered.

Everything about how OptiFuelUK works, what it costs, and where the prices come from.

Pricing
How much does OptiFuelUK cost?
OptiFuelUK is a one-time £1.99 on the App Store — no ads and no subscription. After that you keep everything you save at the pump.
Is there a subscription or in-app purchase?
No. It's a single £1.99 up front — no subscription and no in-app purchases. Route planning, ranked stations, multiple vehicles and the lifetime savings tracker are all included.
How it works
Why isn't the cheapest price always the best?
A station a few pence cheaper per litre might sit several miles off your route. Once you count the fuel you burn driving there and back, that detour can cost more than it saves. OptiFuelUK ranks by total cost — fuel plus detour-fuel-burn — so the genuinely cheapest option rises to the top.
What is the price-decile colour scale?
Stations are coloured by price decile: green is the cheapest 10%, red is the priciest 10%, with a smooth scale between. Map pins and list dots use the same colour, so a result row links visually to its pin.
Can I change between miles and kilometres?
Yes. Settings lets you pick miles or kilometres, set your default search-corridor width, and choose how fresh a price must be to count in the rankings.
Does it work for diesel and different cars?
Yes. Save multiple vehicle profiles with their own fuel type, efficiency and tank size. The active vehicle drives the cost maths, so the rankings always match the car you're actually driving.
Data & coverage
Where do the prices come from?
From the UK Government Fuel Finder open API, under which every UK retailer must publish price changes within 30 minutes. OptiFuelUK is unaffiliated with the UK Government — it just consumes the open data under the Open Government Licence v3.0.
How fresh are the prices?
Retailers are required to publish changes within 30 minutes. You can set a freshness window in Settings — any station whose price hasn't updated within it is left out of the rankings.
Which areas does it cover?
The UK, wherever retailers publish to the Fuel Finder scheme. Plan a route anywhere in the country and OptiFuelUK ranks the stations within your chosen corridor.
Privacy & platform
What does it do with my location?
Your location is used only to set your start point and find nearby stations. OptiFuelUK is built entirely on Apple frameworks with zero third-party SDKs, so your routes stay on your device.
Which devices does it run on?
OptiFuelUK is a native iOS app, built in SwiftUI for iPhone. Download it from the App Store.
