The detour maths

A station 1p cheaper, 5 km off-route, can cost you more.

Most apps rank by pence-per-litre. OptiFuelUK costs the whole picture — including the fuel you burn getting there and back.

The pricier-per-litre station wins by £1.02 — because the cheaper one's detour quietly added more than it saved. OptiFuelUK does this maths for every station along your route, automatically.

How it works

Three taps to a cheaper tank.

1

Plan your route

Enter where you're starting and where you're headed. Use your current location with one tap.

2

See stations ranked

OptiFuelUK finds every station within your search corridor and ranks them by true total cost.

3

Navigate & save

Tap to open Apple Maps, Google Maps or Waze — and your saving is logged to Journeys.

Journeys

Watch your savings add up.

Every trip you navigate from OptiFuelUK logs its saving versus the baseline station. Your lifetime total sits at the top, like a running balance — proof the detour maths pays off.
