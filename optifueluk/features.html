Features

Built to cost the whole journey, not just the price board.

A native iOS app that ranks every station on your route by what it'll actually cost you to fill up there.

True total cost

Fuel plus the fuel you'd burn on the detour, costed at each station's own price. The cheapest total wins — not the cheapest sticker.

Price-decile colours

Map pins and result dots share one scale, green for the cheapest 10% through to red for the priciest — so a row links instantly to its pin.

Detour-aware routing

Set how far either side of your route to look. OptiFuelUK measures the real distance and time off-route for every option.

Live prices, streamed in

Results slot into place cheapest-first as live prices arrive — no waiting for the full list to load before you see the winner.

Multiple vehicles

Save each car, van or fuel type with its own efficiency and tank. The active vehicle drives the cost maths.

Lifetime savings tracker

Every navigated trip logs its saving to Journeys, totted up into a running balance that proves the maths pays off.

Four tabs

Everything in its place.

Plan route

Enter start and end with address autocomplete, or tap to use your current location. See stations colour-coded along your corridor, then push into the ranked results.

Journeys

A lifetime-savings tracker. Every exported trip adds its saving-versus-baseline to a running total, shown as a big rounded balance at the top.

Vehicles

Keep multiple saved profiles — fuel type, efficiency, tank size and current level. Switch the active one and every ranking re-costs to match.

Settings

Choose miles or kilometres, set your default search-corridor width, and control how fresh a price must be to count in the rankings.

The data

Real prices, straight from the source.

OptiFuelUK uses the UK Government Fuel Finder open API, under which every retailer must publish price changes within 30 minutes. No third-party middlemen, no stale crowd-sourced guesses.
