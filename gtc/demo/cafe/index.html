OUR STORY

A corner of the neighbourhood worth slowing down for.

Maple Street started with one roaster, a hand-painted sign and a simple idea: serve the kind of coffee you'd cross town for, in a room you never want to leave.

Today we roast every bean ourselves, bake before sunrise, and know most of our regulars by name — and by their order.
